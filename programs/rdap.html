Course Descriptions

1. Statistics for Research

+
Core Theory 3 Weeks (Month 1)

Topics Covered:

  • Descriptive statistics and data visualization
  • Probability theory and distributions
  • Hypothesis testing and confidence intervals
  • Statistical inference and interpretation

2. SPSS for Research Data Analysis

+
Core Skill Across 4 Months

Topics Covered:

  • Data entry and variable management
  • Data cleaning and transformation
  • Statistical analysis procedures
  • Output interpretation and reporting

3. Research Methods and Design

+
Short Modular 2 Weeks (Month 2)

Topics Covered:

  • Research design types and selection
  • Sampling methods and techniques
  • Measurement and validity
  • Research ethics and integrity

4. Research Writing and Presentation

+
Short Modular 1 Week (Month 2)

Topics Covered:

  • Academic writing structure and style
  • Results reporting and interpretation
  • MS Word formatting for research
  • PowerPoint presentation skills

5. Introduction to R Programming for Research

+
Advanced Tool 2 Weeks (Month 4)

Topics Covered:

  • R fundamentals and syntax
  • Data handling and manipulation
  • Basic statistical analysis in R
  • Visualization with ggplot2

6. Cross-Program Seminar Courses

+
Seminars Invited Professionals

Topics Covered:

  • Responsible Use of AI in Research
  • Digital & Data Thinking
  • Data Visualization & Interpretation
